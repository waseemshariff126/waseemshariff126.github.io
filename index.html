Biography & Research Interests

Originally from Bengaluru, India, Waseem Shariff holds a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Nagarjuna College of Engineering and Technology (2019) and a master's degree in Computer Science specializing in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Galway (2020). He is currently pursuing his PhD at the University of Galway, focusing on machine learning for computer vision applications in automotive driver monitoring systems.
